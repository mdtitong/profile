About me

Mark Dave Titong recently obtained his Bachelors Degree in Computer Science from Thomas Edison State University. He has a solid foundation in object-oriented programming, and is still eagerly continuing to learn new technologies to be able to grow, adapt and be an expert in his future career.

He previously worked as a healthcare employee but made a decision to finally pursue his passion for coding. There is not much to relate in his previous job to being a software engineer, however, other aspects such as being a team player, problem-solver, and customer service are strengths he developed that are also essential to a software engineering career. His computer science related coursework during his college journey coupled with constant self-teaching allowed him to create projects that showcased his passion for learning and can also be added to his portfolio for programming applications experience.

Being able to finish college is one of his biggest accomplishments. Trials and challenges affected the pace of his dream of becoming a software engineer but he did not give up. Now that he just became a father, it drives him to be more motivated in achieving his career goals.
